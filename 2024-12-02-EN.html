Design key features


The main goal of kode dot is to pack the most advanced microprocessor and sensor technologies into a compact device (smaller than an Arduino). All of this is designed to be the perfect foundation for any electronics project.


Imagine holding a device that combines the best of current and future technology, ready to power all your most innovative projects.


Here’s a look at the key decisions we’ve made in the design:


  • ESP32-S3 Microprocessor: Powerful, efficient, and capable of handling even the most demanding projects.


  • Integrated Key Sensors: Built-in IMU, microphone, and speaker, perfect for AI and IoT projects.


  • Touchscreen: Take your projects to the next level with an intuitive and dynamic user interface.


  • More than 10 Programmable GPIOs: Connect and control all kinds of sensors, actuators, and devices.


  • Built-in Battery: Say goodbye to wires! Power your projects directly from the device.


  • Full Connectivity: With USB, UART, I2C, and more ports, you can easily integrate any peripheral.


In short, what we've created is an evolution of current development platforms, geared toward the technologies of the future (which are already here).


This is the device that will give you everything you need to innovate and create without limits!
